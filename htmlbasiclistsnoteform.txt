  <p>Woah, a lot of tables were just presented in front of us, let's go over all the information and make a 'table' review</p>
  <ul>
   <li>The 'table' markup is used to start a table while the '/table' markup is used to end a table</li>
   <li>The 'caption' markup is used for the title of the table</li>
   <li>The 'tr' markup is to start every row (table row)</li>
   <li>The 'th' markup is used for a table heading</li>
   <li>The 'td' markup is for ordinary data included in the table (table data)</li>
   <li>'colspan' is used for adjusting the horizontal lengths of a cell (column span)</li>
   <li>Last but not least the 'rowspan' markup is used for adjusting the vertical heights of a cell (row span)</li>
  </ul>
